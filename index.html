Omnifood

Omnifood is a sleek food ordering app website created with HTML5 and CSS3, boasting a modern single-page layout. The addition of JavaScript enhances functionality, with the use of intersection observer ensuring smooth scrolling throughout the site. A distinctive feature is the incorporation of both dark mode and light mode, providing users with a personalized visual experience. The website's responsive design ensures accessibility across various devices, making Omnifood a user-friendly and stylish platform for food enthusiasts.
